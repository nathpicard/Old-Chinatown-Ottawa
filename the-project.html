How "Ottawa's Old Chinatown" come to be?

Nathalie Picard has never lived in Ottawa before September 2017. She has also never build a website or used any kind of code. ever.


Luckily, other people did the work for her! This is a project that truly owes itself to those that came before it, probably more than most other projects do. In researching Chinatown in Ottawa, I came across so many important volumes that collected the information on Ottawa's first chinese families that I would never have had the time to collect myself. At the same time, I was able to put this site together through useful tutorials online and a sheer will to copy and paste every piece of code necessary. It also took a lot of wonky looking websites, and it will likely look wonky again!


Finally, this project is a scam. The photos and the videos produce are not my own, nor do I have permission to use them. Yet, here they are, detailing a history that I believe is important to tell. It's a guerilla history, but guerilla against whom?
